\# StockFlow AI\## Project OverviewStockFlow AI is an AI-powered Inventory Management SaaS platform designed for small and medium businesses.The platform enables businesses to manage products, inventory, suppliers, customers, warehouses, purchases, sales, invoices, reports, and business analytics from one place.The system will also provide AI-powered insights such as demand forecasting, intelligent stock recommendations, and business analytics.\---\## Objectives\- Replace Excel-based inventory management\- Reduce manual work\- Improve inventory accuracy\- Automate reports\- Provide AI-powered business insights\- Improve productivity\---\## Target Users\- Shop Owners\- Warehouse Managers\- Sales Executives\- Accountants\- Small \& Medium Businesses\---\## Expected OutcomeA modern, scalable, secure web application that businesses can use every day.
