\# StockFlow AI



\## Project Overview



StockFlow AI is a cloud-based Inventory Management SaaS platform designed for small and medium businesses.



The platform helps businesses manage:



\- Products

\- Inventory

\- Suppliers

\- Customers

\- Sales

\- Purchases

\- Warehouses

\- Reports

\- Employees



The platform also provides AI-powered features such as:



\- Demand Forecasting

\- Smart Reorder Suggestions

\- Business Analytics

\- AI Business Assistant



\---



\## Mission



To simplify inventory management for businesses by providing a modern, secure, intelligent, and easy-to-use platform.



\---



\## Vision



To become an all-in-one inventory and business management platform powered by Artificial Intelligence.
