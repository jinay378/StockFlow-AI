\# Project Vision



\## Vision



To become one of the most reliable AI-powered inventory management platforms for small and medium businesses.



\---



\## Mission



Build software that helps businesses manage inventory faster, smarter, and more efficiently using Artificial Intelligence.



\---

Expand StockFlow AI into a complete business management ecosystem including CRM, accounting, warehouse management, AI assistants, and business intelligence.

\- Reliability

\- Security

\- Performance

\- Scalability

\- Innovation
